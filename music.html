MICHAEL MANWARING

KNEEDE'S BRIDGE

I wrote this song while I was living in Arkansas, recorded in Chicago. The title is a tongue-in-cheek reference to the fact that this song has no written bridge section; but as you can hear, one has been improvised to a substantial length.

HOLLY JOLLY CHRISTMAS

Last Christmas, Andrew and I got together to record this verion of Burl Ives classic christmas song. What it lacks in elaborate production it makes up for with earnest holiday joy and the sound of real fire crackling away in the background.
